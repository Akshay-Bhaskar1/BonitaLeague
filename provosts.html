It all started on ONE FATEFUL DAY, when Akshay (admittedly off-task) decided to send Shayan an email alongside Jay, outlining the basics of what would eventually become the Bonita Basketball League. The three friends took the help of Provost Afnan to turn what was just a few emails into a league that has inspired countless other leagues to pop up all around the Plano area. We hope the league will continue to be a beacon of fun for everyone, and as always, GO BALLS!!!
