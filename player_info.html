Player of the Week

This week's player is Omkar Vasa

Whether in good times or bad, Omkar Vasa had always stepped it up. The player of the week averaged 9.5 points per game - leading the league. His most impressive performance last week was his 12 points against the Ashwinners, carrying the Green Goblin Gooners to a 19-0 victory. In addition, he racked up 2 rebounds and 1 assist. GO BALLS!
